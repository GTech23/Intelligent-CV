Intelligent CV is a modern, responsive web application designed to help users create professional, job-winning resumes in minutes. Leveraging the power of AI, this tool provides smart suggestions for skills and summaries, offers a variety of professionally designed templates, and guides users through a seamless, step-by-step process to build a standout resume.

## ‚ú® Features

-   **AI-Powered Suggestions**: Get intelligent recommendations for skills, professional summaries, and job duties tailored to your desired role.
-   **Professionally Designed Templates**: Choose from a collection of modern, HR-approved templates to make your resume stand out.
-   **Step-by-Step Builder**: An intuitive, multi-step form guides you through filling out every essential section of your resume, from contact information to professional references.
-   **Real-time Preview**: Instantly see how your resume looks as you fill in your details, ensuring a perfect final product.
-   **Rich Text Editing**: Utilize a clean, powerful text editor to format your responsibilities and summaries with ease.
-   **Secure Authentication**: A complete user authentication system (Register, Login, Forgot Password) to manage your resume data securely.
-   **PDF Download**: Download your completed resume as a high-quality PDF, ready to be sent to recruiters.

## üöÄ Usage

This application is designed to be straightforward and user-friendly. Here‚Äôs how to get started:

1.  **Create an Account**: Register for a new account or log in if you already have one.
2.  **Choose a Template**: Browse through the available resume templates and select the one that best fits your style and industry.
3.  **Fill in Your Details**: Follow the guided steps in the sidebar to enter your information:
    -   **Contact**: Basic personal and contact information.
    -   **Experience**: Detail your work history.
    -   **Education**: List your academic qualifications.
    -   **Skills**: Add relevant skills, with AI suggestions to help you.
    -   **Summary**: Craft a compelling professional summary, enhanced by AI-generated options.
    -   **Certifications & References**: Include any additional qualifications or professional contacts.
4.  **Finalize and Preview**: Once all sections are complete, you can preview your final resume to ensure everything is perfect.
5.  **Download**: Download your polished, professional resume as a PDF file and start applying for jobs!
